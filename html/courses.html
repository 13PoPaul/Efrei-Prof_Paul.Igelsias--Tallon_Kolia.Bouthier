Computer Science Curriculum

The Efrei Computer Science Department offers a comprehensive and dynamic curriculum designed to prepare future engineers for the rapidly evolving tech industry. Our teaching philosophy blends strong theoretical foundations with intensive, hands-on practical projects.

From fundamental algorithms and object-oriented programming to advanced artificial intelligence and complex system architecture, our courses cover the full spectrum of modern computer science.

Core Study Tracks & Majors

Discover the main disciplines and technical specializations covered throughout our engineering program.

💻

Software Engineering

Master modern programming languages (Java, C++, Python, JavaScript), web & mobile development, and advanced software architecture principles.

🧠

Data & Artificial Intelligence

Dive into machine learning, deep learning, data visualization, and big data management to build intelligent, data-driven systems.

🔐

Cybersecurity

Learn to protect information systems through ethical hacking, network security protocols, cryptography, and risk management strategies.

☁️

Cloud & Network Infrastructure

Understand modern infrastructure by studying cloud computing architectures, IoT, network administration, and DevOps practices.
